############################################
# POKÉMON: SHADOWS OVER KANTO - STORYLINE
############################################

TITLE: Pokémon: Shadows Over Kanto
REGION: Kanto
MAIN_CHARACTER: Ash Ketchum
RIVAL: Gary Oak
VILLAIN_TEAM: Team Umbra
MAIN_LEGENDARY: Mewtwo Umbra
PROFESSOR: Professor Oak

############################################
# CHAPTER 1: A NEW JOURNEY BEGINS
############################################
LOCATION: Pallet Town → Route 1 → Viridian City → Pewter City
GOAL: Receive your first Pokémon and earn your first Gym Badge.
EVENTS:
- Meet Professor Oak and choose your starter Pokémon.
- Battle your rival Gary for the first time.
- Travel to Pewter City and challenge Brock for the Boulder Badge.

############################################
# CHAPTER 2: ELECTRIC TROUBLE IN CERULEAN
############################################
LOCATION: Cerulean City
GOAL: Earn your second Gym Badge and stop Team Umbra’s first heist.
EVENTS:
- Battle Misty for the Cascade Badge.
- Team Umbra steals a mysterious device from the Pokémon Center.
- Gary helps you stop them but fails — vows to become stronger.
SIDEQUEST: Help the Bike Shop owner recover stolen parts.

############################################
# CHAPTER 3: SHADOWS AT SEA
############################################
LOCATION: Vermilion City
GOAL: Defeat Lt. Surge and uncover Team Umbra’s next plan.
EVENTS:
- Board the S.S. Anne and battle Umbra Grunts collecting data on Electric Pokémon.
- Defeat Lt. Surge for the Thunder Badge.
SIDEQUEST: Retrieve the Captain’s lost Poké Flute.

############################################
# CHAPTER 4: GHOSTS OF LAVENDER
############################################
LOCATION: Lavender Town
GOAL: Discover Team Umbra’s dark experiments with Pokémon souls.
EVENTS:
- Explore the Pokémon Tower and calm the spirits.
- Face Umbra Commander Noctis for the first time.
- Rescue a possessed Cubone.
SIDEQUEST: Help a young trainer give peace to his Cubone’s spirit.

############################################
# CHAPTER 5: THE ROAD TO INDIGO
############################################
LOCATION: Celadon, Fuchsia, Saffron, Cinnabar
GOAL: Collect the remaining Gym Badges and uncover Umbra’s true goal.
EVENTS:
- Gary is kidnapped by Umbra; infiltrate Silph Co. to save him.
- Learn about Mewtwo Umbra’s existence.
- Face Erika, Koga, Sabrina, and Blaine to collect all 8 Badges.
SIDEQUEST: Prevent a volcano eruption with Blaine on Cinnabar Island.

############################################
# CHAPTER 6: THE INDIGO TOURNAMENT
############################################
LOCATION: Indigo Plateau
GOAL: Compete in the Pokémon League Tournament.
EVENTS:
- Battle your way through trainers.
- During the opening ceremony, Team Umbra attacks.
- Mewtwo Umbra appears, under their control.
- Final battle: Ash + Gary vs. Noctis + Mewtwo Umbra.
OUTCOME: Mewtwo breaks free and disappears. Peace returns to Kanto.

############################################
# SIDEQUESTS (Optional)
############################################
- Fossil collector in Mt. Moon: recover ancient Pokémon remains.
- Bill’s experiment gone wrong: help him revert to human form.
- Collect Shadow Shards to weaken Mewtwo Umbra later.

############################################
# ENDING
############################################
- Ash and Gary stand on the ruins of the arena.
- Professor Oak congratulates them both.
- The sun rises over Kanto.
- “A new journey awaits...”
